Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how FieldStack ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to synchronize transaction data between your SQL Server database and QuickBooks Online.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 QuickBooks Data

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we collect and store:

  • QuickBooks Company ID (Realm ID)
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens (encrypted)
  • Chart of accounts information
  • Customer and vendor lists
  • Class and department information
  • Journal entry data created by the sync process

2.2 Transaction Data

We access transaction data from your SQL Server database including:

  • Transaction dates and amounts
  • Store and location information
  • Transaction types and categories
  • Payment methods

2.3 Application Usage Data

We collect operational data to ensure the application functions correctly:

  • Sync history and batch information
  • Error logs and diagnostic information
  • Application performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Synchronization: To read transaction data from your database and create journal entries in QuickBooks Online
  • Mapping: To maintain mappings between your data and QuickBooks accounts, classes, and customers
  • Monitoring: To track sync status, detect errors, and ensure data integrity
  • Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide technical assistance

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

All data is stored on your organization's servers or cloud infrastructure. We do not transfer your data to third-party servers except as required for QuickBooks API communication.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All QuickBooks OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • SQL Server connections use encrypted channels
  • Access control and authentication via JWT tokens
  • Security headers via Helmet.js
  • Regular security updates and patches

4.3 Data Retention

Sync history and logs are retained locally on your server. QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored as long as your QuickBooks connection remains active. You can revoke access at any time through the QuickBooks Developer Portal or by disconnecting within the application.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Intuit/QuickBooks: We share transaction data with QuickBooks Online as required to create journal entries via their official API
  • Error Monitoring: If configured, error logs may be sent to Sentry.io for monitoring purposes (sensitive data is filtered out)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the application
  • Review sync history and mapped data
  • Modify or delete mappings
  • Disconnect from QuickBooks at any time
  • Export sync history data

6.2 QuickBooks Connection

You can revoke the application's access to QuickBooks at any time through:

  • The application's settings page
  • QuickBooks Online account settings
  • Intuit Developer Portal

6.3 Data Deletion

To delete your data, you can:

  • Delete the application's database file (sync-history.db)
  • Uninstall the application
  • Contact your system administrator

Note: Deleting data from the application does not delete journal entries already created in QuickBooks. Those must be managed through QuickBooks Online directly.

7. QuickBooks Integration

This application integrates with QuickBooks Online using Intuit's official OAuth 2.0 API. When you connect to QuickBooks:

  • You are redirected to Intuit's secure login page
  • We never see or store your QuickBooks password
  • You explicitly authorize what data we can access
  • You can revoke access at any time

Intuit's privacy policy applies to data handled by QuickBooks: https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/

8. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for business use only and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact your system administrator or the application developer.

In Plain English: This application syncs your transaction data from SQL Server to QuickBooks. We only access data necessary for synchronization, we don't share it with anyone except QuickBooks (as required for the sync), all data stays on your servers, and you can disconnect at any time. We take security seriously and use industry-standard encryption and security practices.
